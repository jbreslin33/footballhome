Team Bio

The Hoboken FC men's second and third teams play in the second divsion and second division reserve of the Cosmopolitan Soccer League, one of the oldest and prestigious amateur soccer leagues in country. The third team plays the same schedule as the second team, with the third match two hours before the second team match.

Under state association registration rules, second and third team players are interchangable and may also be called up to play first team matches.
