Whether you're a captain or coach looking to organize your roster, a player seeking detailed league rules, or someone needing access to dispute forms and other essential resources, you'll find it all here. We've compiled all the tools and information in one place to help you focus on what matters most—enjoying the game and building your team’s success.

Game Day Disputes Form

Complete this form within 24 hours of the final whistle of the game. Any forms submitted after 24 hours will not be considered and the referee's decision or result of the match will stand.

Safe Space Form

This form is used to provide CASA members a safe space to file a complaint about any CASA-related incident *outside* of game-day disputes. The information you provide will only be seen by our Dispute Committee. We take these matters very seriously. (This submission is completely anonymous unless you fill out the entire form)
